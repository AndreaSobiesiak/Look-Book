Our Story

Paws & Play is a family-owned and operated business, proudly serving dogs for over a decade. We are dedicated to providing your dog with the best experience possible here at the farm. Paws & Play is more than just a daycare—we offer a full day of socializing, physical activity, and play. Your dog will have the chance to interact with and bond with other dogs of similar size, fostering essential socialization skills. This interaction is vital for your dog’s health, and Paws & Play offers the perfect environment for your pup to make lifelong friends. Our team of dog experts ensures your dog’s safety while guaranteeing they have the time of their life here at the farm!
